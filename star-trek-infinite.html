Star Trek: Infinite

A grand strategy experience that lets you play your own Star Trek story as the leader of one of four major factions in the galaxy. Follow the specially crafted story or blaze your own trail.

View on Steam emoji_events Best Narrative 2024

Game Overview

Star Trek: Infinite puts you in the captain's seat of one of four legendary powers—Federation, Klingons, Romulans, or Cardassians—and invites you to build your own spacefaring story.

Explore and settle: Send your ships into uncharted systems, found colonies on distant planets, and manage vital resources like dilithium and tritanium.

Shape your narrative: Each faction follows its own mission path, offering events drawn from Trek lore and new twists that reflect your decisions.

Choose your strategy: Cultivate alliances and trade, engage in diplomatic talks, or deploy your fleet for tactical conflicts—your approach defines the outcome.

Mission Tree

The Mission Tree serves as the backbone of narrative and strategic progression. I designed and documented this system to translate complex lore into clear, engaging objectives that reflect each faction's unique identity.

Overview

  • Designed and structured the Mission Tree for multiple factions.
  • Translated Star Trek canon into accessible gameplay objectives.
  • Balanced rewards, pacing, and player progression.
  • Collaborated with narrative, UI/UX, and programming teams.

Process

  • Researched franchise canon and analyzed 4X mechanics.
  • Structured mission branches, unlock conditions, and rewards.
  • Iterated on pacing based on QA feedback and playtesting.

Narrative Events

Narrative Events transform iconic Star Trek moments into interactive gameplay. I contribute to the creation of major story-driven events that give weight to player decisions and shape the overall experience.

Overview

  • Design of branching narrative events tied to faction progression.
  • Highlight: the Romulan supernova event, adapted into a dramatic gameplay moment.
  • Collaboration with narrative and systems teams to ensure immersion and consistency.

Impact

Narrative events immerse players in the Star Trek universe by connecting lore with gameplay. They add drama, consequence, and replayability to faction storylines.

Achievements

Achievements align player milestones with progression, replayability, and narrative goals. I design the entire Achievement system, ensuring milestones feel rewarding and meaningful.

Overview

  • Creation of all Achievements for the game.
  • Alignment of achievements with Mission Tree progression and key events.
  • Design of triggers and conditions that reinforce meaningful milestones.
  • Balance between accessibility for new players and challenges for veterans.

Impact

The Achievement system motivates diverse playstyles and enhances replayability, while also celebrating key narrative and gameplay milestones.
