Game Overview

Outbreak: Dead City is a prototype of a systemic survival game set in a collapsing city. The current slice highlights resource gathering, weapon swapping, modular base upgrades, and an AI system that creates tension through dynamic states.

Pickable resources: Gather supplies scattered across the city that feed directly into crafting, upgrades, and survival progression.

Weapon swapping: Unlock and equip multiple weapons, adapting to scarcity and enemy encounters by switching loadouts on the fly.

Enemy AI: Patrol - Chase - Roam behavior cycles that create dynamic windows for evasion, stealth, and counterattacks.

Modular upgradeable base: A safe hub for storing resources, unlocking modules, and strengthening defenses, serving as the anchor of long-term progression.

Persistent save system: Resources and equipped weapons are remembered across sessions, ensuring continuity and meaningful progress.

Interaction prompts: Contextual UI prompts guide the player through actions, making survival decisions intuitive and responsive.
